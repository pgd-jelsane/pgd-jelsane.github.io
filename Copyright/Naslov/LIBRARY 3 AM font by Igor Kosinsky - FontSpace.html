Library 3 AM one line font. It comes with two styles - regular and soft, supports Cyrillic and multilanguage. Great for logos, posters, headlines, signage, neon titles, print media and more.

Library 3 AM - free font for personal and commercial use. Any donation are very appreciated.

Paypal account for donation: pjms@mail.ru

Thank you.
